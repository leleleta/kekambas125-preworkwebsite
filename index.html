About Me

My name is Lele, I am originally from Columbus, Ohio but my ethinictiy is Ethiopian.

Growing up I was a happy child with so much energy and always finding something to do. My dad was always throwing me into different programs and activites, anything that peaked my interest. Similar to why I decided to get into software devloping, I heard about it through family and thought hmm seems interesting. Once I looked more into it I realized it kind of reminds me of my hardest math and science classes. The challenges I found in my AP Calcus or Chemistry classes have prepared me for this in a kind of way. Whenever I was faced with a problem I couldn't solve, I always went the extra mile to figure it out. Whether that was coming in before class or staying after to ask my teachers for help and I believe that on its own has taught me to speak up when needed and to look for extra resources to help myself when needed. That goes to say I am very excited for this new journey/challenge of this course and to be able to push myself even more!
